onContextTableSelect

PURPOSE

selects or deselects whole drainage and imbibition

SYNOPSIS

function onContextTableSelect(src,~)

DESCRIPTION 

onContextTableSelect selects or deselects whole drainage and imbibition
data in NUCLEUSmod

 Syntax:

       none
