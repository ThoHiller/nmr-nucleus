getShapeFactor

PURPOSE

calculates he shape factor G according to G = A / P^2

SYNOPSIS

function G = getShapeFactor(A,P)

DESCRIPTION 

getShapeFactor calculates he shape factor G according to G = A / P^2

 Syntax:
       getShapeFactor(A,P)

 Inputs:
       A - area
       P - perimeter

 Outputs:
       G - shape factor

 Example:
       G = getShapeFactor(A,P)

 Other m-files required:
